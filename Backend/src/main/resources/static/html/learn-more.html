About Our Project

QVentor is an innovative inventory management solution designed to simplify stock tracking for businesses of all sizes. Using advanced QR code technology, we ensure seamless, accurate, and real-time monitoring of your inventory. Our platform allows quick product scanning, instant updates, and smart analytics to optimize your business operations.

With QVentor, you get a user-friendly interface, high data security, and the ability to integrate with existing systems effortlessly. Whether you run a small shop or a large warehouse, our solution keeps you in control at all times.

Our 3-Type Authentication System

To ensure maximum security and prevent unauthorized access, QVentor uses a 3-Type Authentication process:

  • 1. Password Authentication: Secure, encrypted password login for all users.
  • 2. QR Code Verification: A unique QR code scan is required for every login session.
  • 3. OTP (One-Time Password): A dynamic OTP is sent to the registered email/phone for final verification.

This triple-layer security ensures that only authorized personnel can access inventory data, protecting your business from any security threats.
